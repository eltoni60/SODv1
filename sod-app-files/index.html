Welcome to Sod!

Sod is a web application that empowers restaurant business owners to create custom point of sale systems.

It was developed to be accessible across all major operating systems, mobile and non-mobile, through the use of web browsers and deployed locally on the business owners restaurant. Slow or non-existing internet will not impeded the day to day functions of the restaurant.
